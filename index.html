🎵 What are Vowels? 🎵

Vowels are SUPER IMPORTANT letters!

The vowels are: A, E, I, O, U

🌟 We find vowels in ALL words!
🌟 Without vowels, we can't make words!
🌟 They are the most special letters in the alphabet!

🔊 Vowels Have Two Sounds! 🔊

Every vowel has TWO different sounds:

📢 SHORT sounds - quick and snappy!
📢 LONG sounds - they say their name!

Today we will learn about the SHORT "A" sound!

The short "a" sounds like "ah" as in "apple" 🍎
🍎 📢 🎵

📖 The Cat's Adventure - Part 1

Once upon a time, there was a friendly cat named Whiskers. Whiskers wore a red hat and a blue cap. One sunny morning, Whiskers sat under a big tree and decided to go on an adventure!
🐱🎩

📖 The Cat's Adventure - Part 2

Whiskers found a map that showed the way to Treasure Island! On the way, he met a friendly bat who was quite fat from eating too many berries. "Let's go together!" said the bat. They decided to tap their feet and dance before continuing their journey.
🗺️🦇💃

📖 The Cat's Adventure - Part 3

After their dance, Whiskers and the bat felt tired. They found a cozy spot and took a nice nap. When they woke up, Whiskers sat on the bat's lap as they looked at the map together. "We're almost there!" they cheered. And they all lived happily ever after! 🌟
😴🏝️✨
